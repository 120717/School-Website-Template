ABOUT US

Quality International School is an educational establishment in the Etoug-Ebe neighborhood in Yaounde, the Capital city of Cameroon. The school, situated at the edge of the middle class residential area and the low in common neighborhood of Etoug-Ebe village, provides a perfect environment for bringing together the needed resources to benefit children from both poor and middle class families.

In Quality International School the young learners believe in an education that affects their hearts to show compassion, their heads to think clearly and make good choices and hands that are always willing to serve the community. We believe that a value based Christian Education is the best preparation for a responsible adulthood.

Please enjoy your visit to our website today. Once you have explored our site, we welcome you to visit our campus in person. You will see firsthand the community in action. See our dynamic classes, walk through our clean campus and witness our exciting and unique educational community. We look forward to seeing you soon.

WHY CHOOSE US

CONDUSIVE CLASSROOMS

Our classrooms are very neat, class sizes not more than 30; good airing in the class as well as sanitation is also well taken care of

TEACHER - STUDENT RELATIONSHIP

Our teacher-student relationship is very strong. Studentsd are let to easily approach teachers for help not only academically but socially and emotionally; every teacher is a friend

Student support and Prep

Each and every student is treated like a child at their home and thus, they are well followed up and prep classes are available for students in need of this

Exta Curricular Activities

We offer Extra curricular activities to build up the students not only academically but also socially.We participate at various international competitions the last of which took some of our students to Ukraine.

Christian Building

We not only take care of the child academically but also Spiritually, through daily class devotions where we have praise, meditation and prayer thirty minutes daily.

Leadership Building

We believe that though students make up 30% of our nation's population, they are 100% of our future. Thus, we help build their leadership skills through mentorship seminars and workshops, escursions and more.
